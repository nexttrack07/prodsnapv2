## Key architecture notes for future me / fresh context

- Capability registry: convex/lib/billing/capabilities.ts. Add new caps here first.
- Plan config: convex/lib/billing/planConfig.ts. Scalar limits (productLimit,
  monthlyCredits) live here, not in Clerk.
- Enforcement helpers: convex/lib/billing/index.ts. Mutations call requireCapability
  / requireCredit / recordCreditUse. All honor BILLING_ENABLED kill switch.
- billingEvents table: doubles as audit log AND monthly-credit ledger. Rows with
  context: 'usage' are the ledger; sum units since startOfMonthUtc() for consumption.
- Counting rule: every generation attempt consumes a credit (retries included).
- Dual kill switch: VITE_BILLING_ENABLED (client build-time, Netlify) and
  BILLING_ENABLED (Convex runtime, no rebuild needed for rollback).
