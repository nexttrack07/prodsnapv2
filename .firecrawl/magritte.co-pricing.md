Questions & answers

How does Magritte work?

Magritte helps you create ads based on proven examples from top brands, with no design experience required. Browse over 30,000 expert-curated ads, filter by topic, industry, or performance, or use AI search to quickly find the perfect match. Once you’ve picked the ad you like, use a pre-designed Canva template to add your branding, update the copy, and make it yours in minutes.

Does Magritte have a free trial?

Yes! You can try any paid plan for free for 7 days.

_Note:_ Some features have limited use during the trial, but you’ll still get a great feel for how Magritte works.

What are weekly ad concepts?

Every Monday, we handpick 20 proven concepts and turn them into ready-to-launch ads tailored to your brand. Powered by AI, guided by manual curation and testing.

How do I use Canva templates?

A Canva template is a ready-to-edit design made by our team in Canva, matching the exact look of top-performing ads from our library. Add your branding, update the copy, and you’re ready to launch. We add 100+ new templates every month.

What are template requests?

If we don’t have a Canva template for the ad you like, you can request one with a single click. We prioritize what our customers ask for most, so no guaranteed ETA, but highly requested ads go to the top of our queue.

How can I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription anytime from your Account page. No questions asked.

Can I get a refund?

Yes, we offer refunds within 3 days of the charge as long as you haven’t used the platform (like using Canva templates). Email artem@magritte.co to request your refund. If eligible, we process most refunds right away, though banks can take up to 1–2 weeks to complete the transfer.
