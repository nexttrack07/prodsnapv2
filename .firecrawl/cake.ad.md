## FAQs

### What is Cake and how does it work?

Cake is a chat-based creative studio that delivers AI-powered ad creatives in seconds — just describe what you need and our generative models handle the design for you.

### What makes Cake's AI-powered ad creatives different from templates or stock generators?

Instead of remixing generic layouts, Cake tailors every visual and headline to the brand DNA (colors, fonts, tone) you import, producing unique, on-brand ads every time.

### How do credits and pricing work?

Each generation uses a clearly displayed credit amount. Pick a monthly plan (Free, Plus, or Flex tiers) and know upfront how many ads you can create — no hidden fees.

### Do I need design experience to use Cake?

No design skills required. Simply chat your idea, and Cake turns it into ready-to-use creative.

### Which ad formats and sizes can Cake export?

Download creatives in JPG, PNG, or WebP sized for major platforms like Meta, Google, and TikTok. Additional formats such as MP4 video are on our roadmap.

### Is my brand data secure?

All logos, images, and brand settings are stored securely and never shared with third parties.

### What if the result isn't exactly what I want?

Refine instantly by chatting new instructions or adjusting the Quality slider to explore fresh variations.

### Is there a free plan to try Cake?

Absolutely. The Free plan lets you test AI-powered ad creative each month before deciding to upgrade.

### Do I need a credit card to test Cake?

Nope. You can use Cake without a credit card. Cake is free until you decide to upgrade your plan.
